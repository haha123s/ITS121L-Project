Embrace the Spirit of Ilocos:

Our restaurant offers a glimpse of Ilocano culture to people who are looking for something more than just cuisine. Decorated with artwork that portrays regional landscapes and customs, "Panganan Iloko" is a voyage into the heart as well as a restaurant. of the Ilocano region. Come experience live music, storytelling sessions, and cultural activities that bring our legacy in daily existence.

Savor the Classics:

Savour signature Ilocano meals like sinanglao, a filling soup packed with the benefits of beef and bile, bagnet, crispy fried pig belly that's a wonderful feast for the senses, and pinakbet, a savoury combination of locally produced veggies cooked to perfection. Every dish pays tribute to our culinary history and is painstakingly made to encapsulate the flavours of the Ilocanos.
