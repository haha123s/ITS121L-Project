In the heart of Ilocos, where culinary traditions run deep and flavors tell tales of heritage, Panganan Ilocos stands as a testament to the passion and perseverance of a family deeply rooted in the art of cooking.

  • Our story begins with a love for traditional Ilocano foods that runs through the veins of our family. Faced with financial challenges, our ancestors turned to their culinary skills, crafting dishes that not only filled bellies but also warmed hearts with nostalgia and tradition.

What started as a humble carinderia, born out of necessity, soon blossomed into a thriving culinary hub. Our grandmother, with her unwavering determination and culinary prowess, turned simple meals into culinary delights that attracted locals and visitors alike.
